1. Types of Partnership
        Simple Partnership: Partners invest capital for the same period.
        Compound Partnership: Partners invest capital for different periods.
2. ratio of division of profits
    when time is same:
    Profit Share = Investment of Partner : Investment of Other Partner(s)

    when time is different
    Profit Share = (Investment × Time) of Partner : (Investment × Time) of Other Partner(s)

3. Working and Sleeping Partners
        Working Partner: Manages the business, may get extra salary/commission.
        Sleeping Partner: Only invests money, does not manage the business.
