Placement-Ready Simple & Compound Interest Questions (30)
==========================================================

I. SIMPLE INTEREST – 15 Placement-Ready Questions
--------------------------------------------------

1. A sum of ₹6000 amounts to ₹7200 in 2 years at simple interest. Find the rate of interest.

2. In how many years will ₹4000 amount to ₹5200 at 10% per annum?

3. What sum of money will earn ₹840 in 4 years at 7% simple interest?

4. At what rate percent will ₹2500 earn ₹500 as interest in 5 years?

5. A sum earns ₹480 interest at 8% per annum in 2 years. What is the principal?

6. The SI on a certain sum for 3 years at 5% p.a. is ₹900. Find the sum.

7. If the SI on a sum for 2 years is ₹300 and the rate is 10%, find the principal.

8. A person lent ₹10,000 at 9% p.a. for 3 years. Find the amount received.

9. A sum of ₹4000 is invested at SI for 2.5 years and earns ₹600. What is the rate?

10. How long will ₹5000 take to amount to ₹5750 at 5% per annum?

11. A certain amount becomes ₹12,500 in 5 years and ₹13,000 in 6 years. Find the principal.

12. ₹8400 was lent at 7.5% per annum for 4 years. Find the interest earned.

13. If SI and principal are equal after 10 years, what is the rate of interest?

14. A sum becomes ₹5500 in 3 years and ₹6100 in 6 years. Find the rate.

15. A man invests ₹7200 at 6% SI p.a. How much will he get after 5 years?
