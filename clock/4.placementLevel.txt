CLOCK PROBLEMS – PLACEMENT LEVEL (MODERATE TO DIFFICULT)

1. At what time between 2 and 3 o’clock will the hands of a clock be together?

2. At what time between 7 and 8 o’clock will the hands of a clock be 60° apart?

3. A clock is 5 minutes slow at 10 a.m. and 5 minutes fast at 3 p.m. When was it correct?

4. Find the time between 4 and 5 when the hands are at right angle for the second time.

5. At what time between 9 and 10 will the hands be in a straight line but not overlapping?

6. A clock gains 3 minutes every hour. If it is set correctly at 5:00 a.m., what time will it show at 11:00 a.m. actual time?

7. The hands of a clock are 180° apart at 6:00. After how many minutes will they be 180° apart again?

8. Find the time between 10 and 11 when the angle between the hands is exactly 45°.

9. A watch gains 4 minutes every 3 hours. If the correct time is 12:00 noon now, what time will it show at 9:00 p.m.?

10. At what time between 12 and 1 is the angle between the hands 90° for the first time?

11. A clock shows 3:20. What is the angle between the hands?

12. How many times in a day (24 hours) are the hands at a right angle?

13. Find the time between 8 and 9 when the hour and minute hands are 100° apart.

14. At what time between 6 and 7 will the hands be together?

15. A clock is 10 minutes fast at 6 p.m. and 5 minutes slow at 6 a.m. the next day. When was it correct?

16. At what time between 2 and 3 is the angle between the hands 90° for the first time?

17. A clock shows correct time at 5:00 a.m. but gains 1 minute every hour. What will be the actual time when it shows 9:00 a.m. the next day?

18. At what time between 1 and 2 o’clock will the hands make an angle of 120°?

19. If a clock is 8 minutes fast at 4 p.m. and 4 minutes slow at 8 p.m., when was it correct?

20. Find the time between 11 and 12 when the hands are exactly 180° apart.
