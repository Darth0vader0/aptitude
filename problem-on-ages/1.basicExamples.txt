PROBLEMS ON AGES – BEGINNER LEVEL QUESTIONS

1. The present age of Ramesh is 24 years. After 5 years, what will be his age?

2. Meena is 4 years older than her brother. If her brother is 10 years old, how old is Meena?

3. The present age of a father is 3 times that of his son. If the son is 8 years old, what is the father's age?

4. A is 5 years younger than B. If B is 20 years old, what is A’s age?

5. The age of a man after 10 years will be 40. What is his present age?

6. The sum of ages of a mother and her daughter is 45 years. If the mother is 30 years old, what is the daughter’s age?

7. The difference between the ages of two brothers is 6 years. If the elder one is 18, what is the age of the younger one?

8. A father is 30 years older than his son. After 10 years, he will be twice as old as his son. Find the present age of the son.

9. The sum of the ages of Amit and his father is 50 years. If Amit’s age is 18 years, what is his father’s age?

10. 5 years ago, the age of a girl was 10 years. What is her present age?

11. The ratio of the present ages of Ravi and Ramu is 3:4. If Ravi is 21 years old, what is Ramu's age?

12. The present age of a mother is twice that of her daughter. If the daughter is 15 years old, what is the mother’s age?

13. Ten years ago, a father was 7 times as old as his son. Now he is 3 times as old. Find the present age of the son.

14. A's age is 4 more than twice B's age. If A is 34 years old, find B’s age.

15. The sum of the ages of a father and his two sons is 80 years. If the sons are 20 and 15 years old, what is the father’s age?

16. Ram is 5 years younger than Shyam. After 7 years, their ages will be in the ratio 4:5. Find the present age of Ram.

17. A person’s present age is one-third of what his age will be 12 years from now. What is his present age?

18. The sum of the ages of a brother and sister is 28. If the brother is 4 years older, find their present ages.

19. After 3 years, the age of a boy will be twice his age 5 years ago. What is his present age?

20. A mother is 3 times as old as her daughter. 5 years ago, the mother was 4 times her daughter’s age. Find the present age of the daughter.
